Welcome to our group!

What we're about

This group is for anyone interested in current technologies, things like cloud computing, agile software development, infrastructure as code, anything that gets your techie gears churning. We'd love to hear what you'd like to present/learn about in our group. We'd like to keep the group open and inviting for people of all skill levels. This group is intended to be group of "generalist" as the community in the area tends to be quite small, we'd like to meet like minds and eventually split into more specialized groups as needed.

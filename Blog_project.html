SEMO General Technology Group

Hello! Welcome to our group!

We are like minded individuals that strive to help others in the programming and technology field around the Southeast Missouri area. We want to build connections and spread our knowledge to each other. We welcome both beginners looking to get into the field and professionals who have knowledge to share. We started the group in April 2018. We do Meetups once a month. Once the group grows, we plan to do more Meetups every month and make more specific topics based on what people want to see and what can be taught.
